# Trust Cascades

## How Social Vouching Creates Funding Momentum

Trust cascades describe how early contributions from close connections create confidence that attracts funding from extended networks and eventually public lenders.

This mirrors traditional lending: you ask family first, then friends, then acquaintances, then institutions.

When someone contributes to your loan, they're doing two things simultaneously:

### 1. Providing Capital

### 2. Vouching for You
Staking their social reputation and signaling trustworthiness to others.

{% hint style="success" %}

A $10 contribution from a mutual friend with ðŸŸ¢ LOW RISK is a **stronger trust signal** than $100 from a stranger with ðŸ”´ HIGH RISK.

## Trust Cascade Mechanics

### Why Trust Cascades Work

#### 1. Peer Monitoring
Group members monitor each other's behavior, reducing moral hazard. If Alice funded your loan, she's incentivized to check in on your progress.

#### 2. Social Sanctions
Network connections create enforcement through reputation damage. Defaulting hurts your relationship with everyone who funded, not just anonymous lenders.

#### 3. Information Cascades
Early endorsements by trusted connections influence subsequent lenders. When Bob sees that Alice (who has 50 mutual friends) funded $30, Bob infers Alice has private information about creditworthiness.

#### 4. Threshold Effects
Once a loan reaches ~30-40% funding from connected lenders, public funding accelerates rapidly. The "tipping point" signals sufficient social validation.

## Network Effects at Scale

As LendFriend grows, trust cascades become more powerful:

### Phase 1: Small Network (Current)
- ~50-200 active users
- Trust cascades within tight communities
- Most loans require 5-10 lenders

### Phase 2: Medium Network (6-12 months)

- Trust cascades across multiple communities
- Cross-network funding becomes common
- Reputation history starts mattering

### Phase 3: Large Network (1-2 years)
- ~50,000+ active users
- Global trust cascades
- Loans fund in hours instead of days
- Strong borrower reputation = instant funding
